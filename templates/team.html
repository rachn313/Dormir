The Team

Rachel Navarrette

Founder

Rachel is a Computer Science Major, Economics Minor (Class of 2021). She is an aspiring product manager, interested in the intersection of design, technology, and business. She hopes to share design thinking and the “entrepreneurship mindset” with the BOW community and give empowerment to women to pursue and achieve their goals. She is the co-founder of Wellesley in Product, an initiative to build a community of Wellesley students interested in product as well as Head of Product & Co-Founder at Project W, a community of BOW womxn interested in entrepreneurship, innovation, and colloboration.



Peggy Wang

Founder

Peggy is a Data Science Major concentrating in Computer Science (Class of 2021). She loves working with data and is particularly interested in the intersection of data, statistics, and technology. She hopes to become a Data Scientist one day, focusing on Machine Learning. She is on the web development team at WHACK (Wellesley College Hackathon) and has worked on Annotation Studio, a document annotation website, in the past, through a UROP at MIT. She is also involved with the Math and Stats departments at Wellesley, as a grader and TA.
